Bodyweight Training Plans

Calisthenics is all about mastering bodyweight exercises through progressive overload. Whether you're a beginner or advanced, here’s a guide to help you level up your skills.

Beginner

Push-ups, squats, bodyweight rows, planks, and lunges.

Intermediate

Dips, pull-ups, L-sits, archer push-ups, and leg raises.

Advanced

Muscle-ups, handstands, front lever, planche, and one-arm push-ups.

Calisthenics FAQ

Can I do calisthenics without equipment?

Yes, many basic calisthenics exercises can be done without any equipment, such as push-ups, squats, and planks.

How do I start with calisthenics?

Start with basic bodyweight exercises like push-ups, squats, and planks, then gradually progress to more advanced movements.

How long will it take to see results?

With consistent training, you should see noticeable results in strength and endurance in 4–8 weeks.
